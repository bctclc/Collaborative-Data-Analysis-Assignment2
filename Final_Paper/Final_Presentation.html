Introduction

  • Climate change, GHG emission

Purpose of Research

  • Identify the characteristics of potential EV buyers
  • Identify the factors encouraging/putting off buying EVs

Data Source

This analysis is based on survey data in the UK called "Opinions and Lifestyle Survey, Electric Vehicles Module, February 2014 and February 2015" conducted by Social Survey Division of the Office for National Statistics. This module comes with two datasets: survey results from 2014 and 2015.

List of Variables

  1. Dependent variables:
  • Interests in EVs
  1. Independent variables:
  • Age, Sex, Income, College degree
  • Drivers licence, Number of Cars
  • Household size, Children
  • Region
  1. Other variables
  • Factors encouraging/putting off buying EVs
  • Cost-related factors encouraging/putting off buying EVs

Conclusion & Recommendations
